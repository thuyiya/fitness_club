Everything Changes

[pause 3s]

Welcome.

[pause 2s]

Take a slow breath in.

[inhale]

[pause 3s]

And gently let it go.

[exhale]

[pause 5s]

Today...

I'd like you to imagine a beautiful flower.

Perhaps it's growing in a quiet garden.

Its petals are soft.

Its colors are bright.

When you first see it...

it feels perfect.

[pause 6s]

You could spend hours admiring it.

Its beauty brings a smile.

Its fragrance fills the air.

It seems as though it could stay this way forever.

[pause 8s]

But every flower...

has its season.

[pause]

Slowly...

almost too slowly to notice...

its petals begin to change.

Its colors soften.

Its leaves become dry.

And one day...

the flower returns to the earth.

[pause 8s]

Was the flower less beautiful...

because it did not last forever?

[pause]

Or was it beautiful...

because it was temporary?

[pause 8s]

Think about a sunset.

No one becomes angry...

because the sun disappears each evening.

We watch it.

We admire it.

And when darkness comes...

we accept it.

Because we understand...

that tomorrow...

the sun will rise again.

[pause 10s]

Life follows the same quiet rhythm.

Every beginning...

has an ending.

Every hello...

is followed by goodbye.

Every child becomes an adult.

Every season changes.

Every day becomes night.

[pause]

This is not something to fear.

It is simply...

how life moves.

[pause 10s]

Sometimes we suffer...

not because life changes...

but because we wish it wouldn't.

[pause]

We want joyful moments...

to stay forever.

We want the people we love...

never to leave.

We want our youth...

our health...

our success...

to remain exactly as they are.

[pause]

Yet life keeps moving.

Like a river.

Never stopping.

Never standing still.

[pause 10s]

Imagine standing beside that river.

You place your hand into the water.

A moment later...

the water has already moved on.

You cannot hold it.

You cannot stop it.

And somehow...

the river is beautiful...

because it continues to flow.

[pause 10s]

Our lives are much the same.

The happiest moments...

pass.

The difficult moments...

also pass.

The person you were five years ago...

is already gone.

And the person you will become...

is quietly growing...

with every breath you take.

[pause 10s]

When we understand this...

something begins to soften.

[pause]

We become more grateful...

because we know this moment is precious.

[pause]

We become more patient...

because difficult times won't last forever.

[pause]

We become kinder...

because everyone we meet...

is carrying something that will also change.

[pause 10s]

There is great freedom...

in expecting change...

instead of resisting it.

[pause]

The heart no longer asks...

"Why is this happening?"

[pause]

Instead it gently asks...

"How can I meet this moment...

with wisdom...

and kindness?"

[pause 10s]

Perhaps there is something in your life...

that you've been struggling to accept.

A change.

A loss.

A disappointment.

A chapter that's ending.

[pause]

For just a moment...

allow yourself to stop fighting it.

[pause]

Take one slow breath.

[inhale]

[pause]

And breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause]

You don't have to like what has happened.

But perhaps...

you can stop carrying the extra weight...

of wishing reality were different.

[pause 12s]

Reality asks only one thing of us.

To meet it.

One breath at a time.

One step at a time.

One day at a time.

[pause 10s]

Notice your breathing.

Each inhale begins.

Each exhale ends.

Neither lasts forever.

And because of that...

another breath is always able to arrive.

[pause 10s]

Life is like breathing.

Holding on too tightly...

creates tension.

Letting life move naturally...

creates space.

[pause 10s]

As you sit here now...

perhaps nothing around you has changed.

The same room.

The same responsibilities.

The same life.

[pause]

Yet something inside you...

can become lighter.

Simply by remembering...

that everything changes.

[pause]

Even this moment.

Even this feeling.

Even this sadness.

[pause 12s]

[soft smile]

Take one final deep breath.

[inhale]

[pause 4s]

And slowly breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 6s]

When you're ready...

gently open your eyes.

Carry this thought with you today.

Not everything lasts forever.

And perhaps...

that is exactly what makes each moment...

so deeply beautiful.

[pause 4s]

May you welcome change...

with courage.

May you meet each ending...

with grace.

And may every new beginning...

find you with an open heart.
