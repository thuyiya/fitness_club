The Circle Has No Beginning

[pause 6s]

Welcome.

There is nothing you need to solve right now.

For these moments...

allow yourself to simply arrive.

[pause 8s]

Take a slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And gently breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

Perhaps your mind has been asking...

"What should I do?"

"Did I make the wrong choice?"

"Am I too late?"

"Will things ever become clear?"

These questions can feel heavy.

But for now...

you do not need to answer them.

[pause 12s]

Imagine a perfect circle.

Smooth.

Continuous.

Without a beginning.

Without an ending.

Look carefully.

Where does it truly begin?

Every point could be the first.

Every point could be the next.

[pause 12s]

Life often feels the same.

We wait for certainty.

For motivation.

For the perfect plan.

Yet life quietly whispers...

Begin where you are.

[pause 12s]

Notice your breathing.

This breath did not wait until you felt ready.

It simply arrived.

You welcomed it.

Then it gently left.

The next breath came on its own.

Life unfolds like this...

one moment at a time.

[pause 15s]

When the mind becomes lost...

it creates many stories.

"What if I fail?"

"What if I chose the wrong road?"

"What if everyone else knows what they are doing?"

Look closely.

These are only thoughts.

They come.

They stay for a while.

Then they leave.

Like clouds crossing an open sky.

[pause 18s]

You are not your thoughts.

You are the quiet awareness noticing them.

Rest here.

[silence]

[pause 35s]

Silently repeat...

May I be patient with myself.

[pause 6s]

May I trust this moment.

[pause 6s]

May I take one gentle step.

[pause 10s]

Nothing more is needed.

One breath.

One kind choice.

One small beginning.


And gently breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

Imagine standing at the edge of a forest.

Many paths appear.

The mind wants certainty.

It wants a guarantee.

Yet no path reveals its whole journey from the beginning.

It reveals itself...

one step at a time.

[pause 15s]

Think of the seasons.

None of them rushes.

None of them fears arriving too late.

Each quietly becomes the next.

Life moves this way too.

Not in straight lines...

but in gentle cycles.

[pause 15s]

Perhaps today you feel behind.

Perhaps someone else seems further ahead.

Pause.

Look only at this breath.

Comparison disappears when attention returns to the present.

[pause 15s]

If you have made mistakes...

allow yourself to learn.

There is no need to carry every yesterday into tomorrow.

A heavy backpack makes every journey harder.

With each exhale...

set one small stone down.

[pause 18s]

You do not need to know your whole future.

You only need enough light for the next step.

Just as a small lantern brightens only a few feet ahead...

yet still guides the entire journey.

[pause 18s]

Silently repeat.

May I release the need for perfect answers.

[pause 6s]

May I meet uncertainty with courage.

[pause 6s]

May I walk gently through life.

[pause 10s]

Now imagine touching any point on the circle.

At that very moment...

it becomes your beginning.

Not because it is special.

But because you are fully present.

[pause 15s]

Rest with your breathing.

[silence]

[pause 45s]

Take one final slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And slowly breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

As you continue your day...

remember this.

You are not falling behind.

You are not broken.

You are growing...

one quiet moment at a time.

Trust the next gentle step.

The circle continues.

And every peaceful breath...

is a beautiful place to begin.
