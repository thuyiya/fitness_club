The Moon Keeper's Garden

[pause]

Welcome...

[pause]

Tonight...

I'd like to take you somewhere...

that only appears...

after the whole world...

has gone to sleep.

[pause]

Take one slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And slowly breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause]

...

[exhale]

Far beyond the tallest mountains...

beyond the clouds...

and beyond the places birds can fly...

there is a quiet garden.

[pause]

No one knows exactly where it is.

No maps show the way.

It simply appears...

to those who need...

a peaceful night's sleep.

[pause]

The garden belongs...

to the Moon Keeper.

[pause]

No one has ever asked...

the Moon Keeper's name.

Because names...

don't matter very much here.

[pause]

Every evening...

just before sunset...

the Moon Keeper walks quietly...

through the garden.

[pause]

There are no flowers.

Instead...

tiny silver moons...

grow upon gentle vines.

Little crescent moons.

Round moons.

Golden moons.

All waiting patiently...

for the evening sky.

[pause]

One by one...

the Moon Keeper...

carefully picks them.

Never rushing.

Never skipping one.

[pause]

Each moon...

is polished gently...

with a soft cloth...

until it shines...

with quiet silver light.

[pause]

Nearby...

thousands of tiny stars...

rest inside woven baskets.

[pause]

Some are bright.

Some are small.

Some sparkle...

just a little more...

than others.

[pause]

Every night...

the Moon Keeper...

places each one...

carefully into the sky.

[pause]

Not because the stars...

cannot do it themselves.

But because...

beautiful things...

deserve patience.

[pause]

Imagine...

walking beside...

the Moon Keeper.

[pause]

Your footsteps...

make almost no sound.

The soft grass...

feels cool beneath your feet.

A gentle breeze...

moves through silver leaves.

Everything...

feels wonderfully calm.

[pause]

The Moon Keeper smiles...

and hands you...

one tiny star.

[pause]

It rests...

warmly...

inside your hands.

Not hot.

Just pleasantly warm.

Almost like...

holding happiness itself.

[pause]

Together...

you gently place the star...

into the evening sky.

[pause]

It floats upward...

slowly...

until it finds...

its perfect place.

[pause]

Not too high.

Not too low.

Exactly where...

it belongs.

[pause]

You notice something wonderful.

No star...

tries to shine...

brighter than another.

[pause]

No moon...

wishes it were larger.

[pause]

No cloud...

tries to move faster.

[pause]

Everything...

simply becomes...

what it was always meant to be.

[pause]

The Moon Keeper notices...

you watching.

Very quietly...

they say...

"The sky has never hurried."

[pause]

"The moon has never worried...

about arriving late."

[pause]

"The stars have never compared...

their light."

[pause]

"And yet...

every night...

the whole sky...

becomes beautiful."

[pause]

You look upward.

Thousands of tiny lights...

shine peacefully.

Each one different.

Each one enough.

[pause]

Perhaps...

people are a little like stars.

[pause]

Some shine early.

Some shine later.

Some shine quietly.

Some brightly.

[pause]

But every light...

has its own season.

Its own place.

Its own time.

[pause]

The Moon Keeper...

gently waters...

the moon garden.

Silver drops...

fall softly...

onto glowing leaves.

Tiny flowers...

made of moonlight...

slowly open.

Fireflies...

dance nearby.

[pause]

Everything grows...

without hurry.

Without pressure.

Without fear.

[pause]

The air...

smells of lavender.

Night jasmine.

Fresh rain.

And cool mountain wind.

[pause]

You find...

a smooth wooden swing...

hanging gently...

from an old silver tree.

[pause]

You sit down.

The swing...

moves ever so slightly.

Back...

and forth.

Back...

and forth.

Like breathing.

[pause]

The Moon Keeper...

continues placing...

tiny stars...

across the sky.

One.

After another.

One.

After another.

Never rushing.

Never stopping.

[pause]

Watching them...

becomes wonderfully sleepy.

[pause]

The stars above...

become softer.

The breeze...

becomes quieter.

The swing...

moves slower.

Your breathing...

slows too.

[pause]

Everything...

begins whispering...

instead of speaking.

[pause]

The Moon Keeper...

lights one final moon.

Its gentle glow...

covers the whole garden...

with soft silver light.

[pause]

Nothing else...

needs doing tonight.

The sky...

is complete.

The stars...

are happy.

The garden...

is peaceful.

[pause]

Perhaps...

you are too.

[pause]

The swing...

moves...

just a little slower.

[pause]

The breeze...

becomes...

even softer.

[pause]

Your body...

becomes heavier.

Warmer.

Safer.

[pause]

The Moon Keeper...

smiles one last time.

Then quietly walks...

among the silver flowers.

Slowly disappearing...

into the peaceful night.

[pause]

Only the stars remain.

Only the moon.

Only your gentle breathing.

[pause]

Everything...

is exactly...

where it belongs.

[pause]

And so are you.

[pause 20s]

[whisper]

Sleep now...

beneath the quiet stars.

[pause]

Tomorrow...

the Moon Keeper...

will prepare the sky again.

But tonight...

the whole universe...

is simply watching over you.

[pause 20s]

Sleep peacefully...

[pause]
