Inviting.

Hidden among the forest...

Its windows glow...

Smoke rises slowly...

from a stone chimney.

The rain taps gently...

against the roof.

[pause]

The door is already open.

As though...

[pause 8s]

Upon them...

Another...

[pause]

No one speaks about them.

[pause]

Gentle.
