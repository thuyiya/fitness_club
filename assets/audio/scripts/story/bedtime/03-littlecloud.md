The Little Cloud Who Couldn't Hurry

[pause]

Hello, little one.

[pause]

high above the hills...

where the sky is peaceful...

and the clouds drift...

ever so slowly.

[pause]

Pull your blanket up.

Let your pillow...

Take one slow breath.

[inhale]

...

[pause]

Its name...

[pause]

Puffy wasn't big.

Puffy wasn't fluffy.

Puffy wasn't fast.

[pause]

[pause]

Puffy tried very hard...

to keep up.

[pause]

"Oh dear,"

"I wish I were bigger."

"I wish I were faster."

[pause]

"I wish I were already grown."

[pause]

The breeze smiled kindly.

"You seem worried."

Puffy nodded.

[pause]

"I think...

I'm growing too slowly."

[pause]

The breeze laughed softly.

Not loudly.

Just enough...

to make the air feel warm.

[pause]

"My little cloud,"

Puffy thought...

[pause]

"No."

"Have you ever seen...

"No."

"Have you ever seen...

the moon...

Puffy smiled.

"No."

[pause]

"Thank you,"

[pause]

Puffy smiled.

Just a little.

Every single day.

[pause]

Just like trees.

Just like children.
