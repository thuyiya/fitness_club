Welcome back.

Take one gentle breath.

[inhale]
...
[exhale]

[pause 10s]

Now let go of counting.
Simply rest with one breath, then the next.

Imagine a calm mountain lake.
When the wind settles, the water becomes still.
The mind is the same.
We do not force stillness.
We allow it.

If thoughts arise, notice them kindly and return to the breath.
Each return builds quiet strength.

[silence]

[pause 60s]

May my mind remain calm.
May my attention become clear.
May I carry this steadiness into every part of my life.

Take one final slow breath.

[inhale]
...
[exhale]

Open your eyes gently.
Carry this quiet focus into your day.
