The Circle Has No Beginning

[pause 3s]

Welcome.

[pause 2s]

Take a slow breath in.

[inhale]

[pause 3s]

And gently breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 5s]

Today...

I'd like you to imagine a simple circle.

Perfectly round.

Smooth.

Continuous.

[pause 6s]

Now ask yourself...

Where does the circle begin?

[pause]

Look carefully.

[pause]

Can you find its first point?

[pause]

Or its last?

[pause 6s]

You can't.

Because every point...

is connected to the next.

Every point...

could be called...

the beginning.

[pause 8s]

Life is much like this circle.

[pause]

Many of us wait...

for the perfect place to begin.

[pause]

"I'll start exercising...

when work becomes easier."

[pause]

"I'll start meditating...

when my mind becomes quieter."

[pause]

"I'll become happy...

when I finally have enough."

[pause]

But the perfect beginning...

never arrives.

[pause 10s]

Imagine a traveler standing before a mountain.

They keep saying...

"I'll begin walking...

when I reach the perfect first step."

[pause]

Yet every path...

begins exactly the same way.

With one ordinary step.

[pause]

Not a perfect step.

Just...

the next one.

[pause 10s]

Think about a tree.

Where did it begin?

Was it the seed?

[pause]

Or the flower...

that created the seed?

[pause]

Or the tree...

that grew before that?

[pause]

Every beginning...

comes from another beginning.

[pause]

Life moves in circles.

Not straight lines.

[pause 10s]

The seasons understand this.

Spring becomes summer.

Summer becomes autumn.

Autumn becomes winter.

Winter quietly becomes spring again.

[pause]

None of the seasons asks...

"When should I begin?"

[pause]

They simply arrive...

when the moment comes.

[pause 10s]

Perhaps your life feels unfinished.

Perhaps you've been waiting...

for motivation.

For confidence.

For certainty.

[pause]

But what if...

confidence isn't the beginning?

[pause]

What if action...

comes first?

[pause]

And confidence quietly follows.

[pause 10s]

Take a slow breath.

[inhale]

[pause]

Notice something.

Did you prepare...

for this breath?

[pause]

Did you need permission?

[pause]

No.

The breath simply arrived.

And you received it.

[pause]

Life often works the same way.

[pause 10s]

Sometimes...

we don't need a new life.

We simply need...

a new beginning.

[pause]

And that beginning...

can happen...

right here.

Right now.

With this breath.

[pause 12s]

Maybe your beginning today...

is drinking one more glass of water.

[pause]

Maybe it's making one phone call.

[pause]

Maybe it's forgiving yourself.

[pause]

Maybe it's resting.

[pause]

Or simply sitting quietly...

for five peaceful minutes.

[pause]

Small beginnings...

change lives.

[pause 12s]

Imagine placing your finger...

anywhere...

on the edge of that circle.

[pause]

The moment you touch it...

that becomes...

the beginning.

[pause]

Not because it was special.

But because...

you decided...

to begin there.

[pause 10s]

Life doesn't ask you...

to begin perfectly.

It only asks...

that you begin.

[pause]

One breath.

One kind choice.

One peaceful thought.

One small step.

[pause]

Again...

and again...

and again.

[pause]

Like the circle...

always moving...

always continuing.

Take one final deep breath.

[inhale]

[exhale]

[pause 6s]

As you open your eyes...

You are never too late.

You are never too early.

You do not need...

the perfect moment.

[pause]

Every moment...

can become...

the beginning...

if you choose it.

[pause 6s]

for perfect timing.
