Welcome back.

Take one slow breath.

[inhale]

...

And gently breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

Imagine holding a handful of soft sand.

With an open hand, it rests peacefully.

When you squeeze tightly, it slips away.

Many parts of life are like this.

Love.

Success.

Youth.

Even happiness.

The tighter we cling, the heavier the heart becomes.

[pause 20s]

Imagine carrying a heavy backpack filled with regret, expectations, and worry.

You have carried it so long it feels like part of you.

Pause.

What if you gently placed it on the ground, just for this moment?

[pause 20s]

Letting go does not mean giving up.

It means no longer fighting reality.

Life moves in a quiet rhythm.

Receiving.

Releasing.

Receiving.

Releasing.

Like every breath.

[pause 20s]

Bring to mind one thing you have been holding too tightly.

Simply loosen your grip.

One breath at a time.

One gentle release at a time.

That is how freedom quietly grows.

[pause 20s]

Silently repeat...

May I have the wisdom to know what I can hold...

and what I must gently release.

[pause 8s]

May my heart remain open through every change.

[pause 10s]

Rest quietly.

[silence]

[pause 45s]

Take one final slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And slowly breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

As you continue your day...

remember that peace grows whenever the heart learns to appreciate without clinging, to receive without possessing, and to love without fear.

May your mind become lighter.

May your heart become wiser.

May each gentle breath remind you that true freedom begins within.

[soft smile]
