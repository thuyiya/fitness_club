The Cup We Keep Filling

[pause 6s]

Welcome.

Allow yourself to arrive with nothing to prove.

Nothing to achieve.

Only this quiet breath.

[pause 10s]

Take a slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And gently breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 12s]

Imagine two people walking through a marketplace.

Both notice the same beautiful object.

One mind whispers,

'I need this to be happy.'

The other quietly smiles, appreciates its beauty, and continues walking.

[pause 15s]

The object never created peace.

The difference was the relationship to it.

Our minds often fill an invisible cup with success, possessions, praise, and recognition.

Yet the cup never seems completely full.

The mind always asks for one more thing.

[pause 18s]

There is nothing wrong with enjoying beautiful things.

The burden begins only when our peace depends on them.

Notice your breathing.

Each breath arrives freely.

Simply rest here.

[silence]

[pause 40s]

Silently repeat...

May I appreciate without clinging.

[pause 6s]

May I enjoy without fear of losing.

[pause 8s]

May my heart discover quiet contentment.

[soft smile]
