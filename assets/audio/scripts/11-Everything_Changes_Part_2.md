Welcome back.

Take one slow breath.

[inhale]

...

And gently breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

Perhaps there is something in your life that is changing.

A relationship.

A season of life.

A dream.

A job.

Your health.

Or perhaps a version of yourself that no longer fits who you are becoming.

[pause 18s]

The mind naturally wants to hold on.

It wants joyful moments to last forever.

It wishes difficult moments had never happened.

Yet both are carried by the same river of time.

Neither can remain unchanged.

[pause 18s]

Imagine standing beside a flowing river.

Place your hand into the water.

A moment later...

that water is already gone.

Not because something is wrong.

But because flowing is the nature of a river.

Change is the nature of life.

[pause 20s]

Notice your own breathing.

Every inhale begins.

Every exhale ends.

If one breath never ended...

another could never begin.

Sometimes letting go is what creates space for new life to enter.

[pause 18s]

If sadness is here...

allow it to be here.

If joy is here...

allow it to be here.

Neither needs to be chased.

Neither needs to be held.

Simply notice.

Everything is moving.

Everything is changing.

[pause 20s]

Silently repeat...

May I meet change with wisdom.

[pause 6s]

May I meet loss with kindness.

[pause 6s]

May I trust that each ending makes room for a new beginning.

[pause 12s]

Rest quietly.

[silence]

[pause 45s]

Take one final slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And slowly breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

As you continue your day...

remember that this moment will never come again.

That is what makes it precious.

Notice the people you love.

Notice the sky above you.

Notice the quiet miracle of another breath.

May your heart become softer with change.

May your mind become lighter with acceptance.

May each new beginning find you ready to receive it.

[soft smile]
