The Quiet Beginning

[pause 5s]

Welcome.

[pause]

There is nothing you need to achieve.

Nothing you need to fix.

Nothing you need to become.

For these moments...

simply allow yourself...

to be here.

[pause 6s]

Feel the gentle support beneath you.

Notice the natural rhythm of your breathing.

Do not change it.

Simply observe.

Like watching gentle waves reach the shore...

and quietly return.

[pause 8s]

Take a slow breath in.

[inhale]

Hold for a gentle moment.

[pause]

Now slowly breathe out.

[exhale]

Long...

soft...

and easy.

[pause 8s]

Again...

[inhale]

...

[exhale]

[pause 10s]

With every breath...

allow the shoulders to soften.

Allow the jaw to relax.

Allow the eyes to rest.

There is nowhere else to be.

[pause 10s]

Now bring your awareness to the center of your chest.

Not searching for anything.

Simply noticing.

Perhaps warmth.

Perhaps stillness.

Perhaps nothing at all.

Whatever you notice...

is enough.

[pause 10s]

Imagine sitting across from yourself.

Not the version others expect.

Not the version you hope to become.

Simply the person who has carried every joy...

every disappointment...

every success...

every quiet struggle.

Look with gentle understanding.

[pause 10s]

Silently repeat...

very slowly...

allowing every sentence to settle deeply.

May I be safe.

[pause 6s]

May I be healthy.

[pause 6s]

May I live with a peaceful heart.

[pause 6s]

May I meet each moment with patience.

[pause 6s]

May I gently release what I cannot change.

[pause 8s]

Notice any resistance.

There is no need to fight it.

Simply notice.

Like clouds drifting across a clear sky...

everything changes.

Everything moves.

[pause 12s]

Rest quietly with your breathing.

[pause 20s]

Every calm breath plants a small seed.

Every gentle thought nourishes it.

In time...

kindness grows naturally.

Not by force...

but through quiet practice.

[pause 15s]

Remain here...

breathing...

resting...

simply being.

[silence]

[pause 30s]

Take one deeper breath.

[inhale]

...

[exhale]

[pause]
[pause 6s]

Bring your awareness once again...

to this gentle breath.

Each inhale arrives on its own.

Each exhale lets go on its own.

Life is unfolding...

one moment at a time.

[pause 10s]

Bring to mind someone who is easy to care for.

See their smile.

Their kindness.

Their humanity.

Silently offer...

May you be safe.

[pause 6s]

May you be healthy.

[pause 6s]

May you know deep peace.

[pause 8s]

Now allow that circle to widen.

Think of someone you hardly know.

Someone you passed today.

Just like you...

they wish to be happy.

They experience hope...

fear...

love...

and loss.

Offer the same quiet wishes.

[pause 10s]

May you be safe.

May you be peaceful.

May your days become lighter.

[pause 12s]

If it feels comfortable...

bring to mind someone difficult.

Not to approve.

Not to excuse.

Only to loosen the tightness inside your own heart.

Holding anger is like carrying a heavy stone.

Setting it down...

does not change the past.

But it changes the one who was carrying it.

[pause 12s]

Silently repeat.

May you find wisdom.

[pause 6s]

May you find peace.

[pause 8s]

Now allow your awareness to expand beyond individuals.

Imagine your heart becoming a warm light.

With every breath...

that light quietly grows.

Across your home.

Across your community.

Across every land.

Touching every life.

Without choosing.

Without excluding.

Simply offering kindness.

[pause 15s]

Silently repeat.

May all beings be safe.

[pause 6s]

May all beings be healthy.

[pause 6s]

May all beings live with peace.

[pause 6s]

May all hearts awaken to kindness and understanding.

[pause 12s]

Now let go of every word.

Simply breathe.

Simply rest.

Notice how each breath appears...

stays for a moment...

and gently disappears.

Thoughts do the same.

Feelings do the same.

Everything changes.

When we stop holding so tightly...

the heart naturally becomes lighter.

[pause 20s]

Rest in this quiet awareness.

[silence]

[pause 45s]

Take one final slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And a long gentle breath out.

[exhale]

[pause 8s]

As you continue your day...

may your words be gentle.

May your actions bring kindness.

May your mind return...

again and again...

to this quiet place within.

[soft smile]

[pause 10s]

Thank you for sharing this peaceful moment.
