The Secret of Enough

[pause 6s]

Welcome.

Allow yourself to settle.

Nothing is missing from this breath.

Nothing needs to be added to this moment.

[pause 10s]

Take one slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And gently breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 12s]

Imagine two people.

One rides an old bicycle through the morning air.

The paint is worn.

The seat is weathered.

Yet each ride fills the heart with quiet joy.

[pause 12s]

Another person travels in a beautiful private jet.

Soft seats.

Wide skies.

Comfort beyond imagination.

Yet after many journeys...

even this becomes ordinary.

[pause 15s]

Notice carefully.

The bicycle never contained happiness.

Neither did the jet.

The feeling came from the mind that experienced them.

[pause 15s]

As children...

a simple cardboard box became a castle.

A spaceship.

A world of adventure.

Nothing changed except the way it was seen.

[pause 15s]

As we grow older...

the mind quietly whispers...

"If only I had one more thing..."

A little more money.

A bigger home.

A newer phone.

More success.

Yet every time we arrive...

the finish line quietly moves again.

[pause 18s]

Rest with your breathing.

Notice that this breath asks for nothing.

It is already complete.

[silence]

[pause 40s]

Silently repeat...

May I appreciate what is already here.

[pause 6s]

May gratitude gently open my heart.

[pause 8s]

May I discover the quiet richness of this moment.

[soft smile]
