Everything Changes

[pause 6s]

Welcome.

Allow yourself to settle into this moment.

There is nothing to hold on to.

Nothing to push away.

Only this gentle breath.

[pause 10s]

Take one slow breath in.

[inhale]

...

And slowly breathe out.

[exhale]

[pause 12s]

Imagine a beautiful flower growing quietly in a peaceful garden.

Its colors are bright.

Its fragrance fills the air.

For a while...

it seems as though this beauty could last forever.

[pause 15s]

Then little by little...

its petals begin to soften.

Its colors gently fade.

One day...

it returns to the earth.

Was it less beautiful because it changed?

Or was its beauty made even more precious because it could not last forever?

[pause 18s]

Now think of a sunset.

No one asks it to remain in the sky forever.

We simply watch.

Appreciate.

And gently allow it to disappear.

Because we trust that another morning will come.

[pause 18s]

Life moves with this same quiet rhythm.

Every beginning becomes an ending.

Every ending quietly becomes another beginning.

Like breathing.

Like the changing seasons.

Like the flowing river.

[pause 18s]

Rest with your breathing.

Notice each inhale appearing.

Each exhale fading away.

Without effort.

Without struggle.

[silence]

[pause 40s]

Silently repeat...

May I welcome each moment as it comes.

[pause 6s]

May I gently release what has already passed.

[pause 8s]

May I trust the quiet rhythm of life.

[soft smile]
