Steady, One-Pointed Attention

[pause 6s]

Welcome.

There is nowhere else you need to be.

Take one slow breath in.

[inhale]
...
[exhale]

[pause 10s]

Notice one small place where the breath is easiest to feel.
Perhaps the tip of the nose.
Perhaps the gentle movement of the chest.

Choose one point.
Let it become your quiet home.

[pause 15s]

The mind will wander.
That is natural.
Each time you notice, gently return.

Now count each out-breath from one to ten.
If you lose the count, simply begin again.
Every return strengthens attention.

[silence]

[pause 45s]

May my mind become steady.
May my attention rest peacefully.
May I return with kindness.
