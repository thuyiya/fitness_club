# Per-session music — ElevenLabs Music prompts

Distinct background music for each of the 5 practice sessions (Breath, Focus,
Relax the Body, Loving-Kindness, Let Go), so they no longer share one bed.

How: ElevenLabs → Music → paste a prompt → set **Instrumental** → max length →
generate → download → rename to the filename shown → drop in `assets/audio/`.
Then these get wired into the session list. Keep all instrumental / no vocals.

## 1 — Breath (Ānāpānasati) → music-breath.mp3
```
Calm ambient meditation music for guided breathing. Warm analog synth pads that swell slowly like a breath, soft felt-piano notes drifting in, a gentle low drone underneath. Instrumental, no vocals, no percussion, around 55 BPM, spacious reverb, welcoming and grounding. Seamless and loopable — music to arrive and settle into.
```

## 2 — Focus (Samatha) → music-focus.mp3
```
Minimal ambient meditation music for deep concentration. A single sustained crystalline drone with a slow, steady singing-bowl pulse, almost no movement, pure clear high harmonics. Instrumental, no vocals, no drums, around 50 BPM, restrained and hypnotic — built to hold steady, one-pointed attention. Seamless and loopable.
```

## 3 — Relax the Body (Kāyagatāsati) → music-body.mp3
```
Deep warm ambient music for a full-body relaxation scan. Heavy low synth pads that slowly descend, soft warm sub-bass, distant bowed strings melting downward, an occasional low soft chime. Instrumental, no vocals, no percussion, around 45 BPM, cocooning and heavy, spa-like — melting tension out of the body. Seamless and loopable.
```

## 4 — Loving-Kindness (Mettā) → music-metta.mp3
```
Tender heart-warming ambient music for a loving-kindness meditation. Soft glowing major-key pads, gentle harp arpeggios, warm cello and a faint music box, compassionate and uplifting. Instrumental, no vocals, no drums, around 55 BPM, glowing and tender, gently emotional. Seamless and loopable.
```

## 5 — Let Go (Open awareness) → music-letgo.mp3
```
Spacious weightless ambient music for open-awareness meditation. Very sparse floating high pads drifting in and out, long airy reverb tails, a few distant echoing tones like light on water, a wide open sense of sky. Instrumental, no vocals, no percussion, around 50 BPM, endless and drifting — for letting thoughts pass. Seamless and loopable.
```
